Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We might already have the answer for you below.

Are HavenMade products handmade?

Yes! Every product is carefully handcrafted in small batches to ensure quality and freshness. No machines, no mass production — just mindful, artisanal care.

Are your soaps suitable for sensitive skin?

Absolutely. Our bars are made with natural oils, butters, and plant-based ingredients that are gentle on all skin types — including sensitive skin.

Do you use any artificial fragrance or color?

No — we only use natural essential oils and herbal infusions for scent and color. Each bar’s tone and aroma come straight from nature.

How can I store my soap for longer use?

Keep it in a dry, well-drained soap dish away from direct water flow. This helps your bar last longer and stay firm.

Do you ship all over India?

Yes, we currently deliver across India. Shipping times vary depending on your location — typically 3–7 business days.

Can I place a bulk or gift order?

Of course! Please email us at havenmadecare@gmail.com with your requirements — we’d love to help you craft custom sets.
